Semantics in K


Featured

  • C (Jul 2010 - Present)

    K semantics of the C programming language. It can help catch the undefined behaviors of C programs.

  • KWasm (Aug 2015 - Present)

    KWasm is the K semantics of WebAssembly.

  • Elrond (Aug 2015 - Present)

    The semantics of Elrond and Mandos.

  • IELE (Oct 2016 - Present)

    Provides a model of IELE in K.

  • KEVM (Sep 2017 - Present)

    KEVM is the K semantics of the Ethereum Virtual Machine. It passes all of the Ethereum Test Suite, and is used for verifying EVM programs.

  • KEwasm (Jul 2019 - Present)

    KEwasm is the K semantics of Ewasm.

  • K-Michelson (Oct 2019 - Present)

    K-Michelson is the K semantics of Michelson blockchain programming language.

  • Boogie (Apr 2020 - Present)

    Presents an executable formal semantics for Boogie, an intermediate language for verification.
